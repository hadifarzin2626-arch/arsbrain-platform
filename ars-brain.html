Independent Automotive Computing Platform

ARS Brain transforms existing vehicles into intelligent, connected and future-ready platforms while preserving their original identity.

What Is ARS Brain?

ARS Brain is not a replacement for the vehicle. It is an independent intelligence layer designed to enhance capability, safety and user experience.

Independent Architecture

ARS Brain operates as an independent computing platform with its own hardware, software and user interface.

No ECU Modification

ARS Brain does not program, modify, flash or interfere with original vehicle ECUs.

OBD-II Integration

Vehicle information is obtained through standard diagnostic interfaces for selected data only.

Modular Platform

Designed for future upgrades, expansion modules and multiple vehicle applications.
