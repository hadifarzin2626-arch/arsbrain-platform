WHY NOW

The Automotive World Is Changing.

Vehicles are becoming increasingly connected, intelligent and software-defined. At the same time, millions of vehicles already on the road were never designed to participate in this new digital era.

01

The Existing Vehicle Fleet

A vast global vehicle population remains in service for many years, creating an opportunity for intelligent retrofit technologies that can extend capability without replacing the entire vehicle.

02

The Rise Of Software

Modern automotive experiences are increasingly shaped by software, connectivity, digital interfaces and continuous updates.

03

The Need For Scalable Intelligence

The next generation of automotive intelligence requires platforms that can evolve continuously rather than remain locked to a single hardware generation.
